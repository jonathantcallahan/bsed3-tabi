Why are you interested in veganism?
People are drawn to veganism for a variety of reasons. There are strongly opinionated divisions both within and without the vegan community about some of them, at the end of the day being informed and knowing what's important to you is all that matters. For many the aspects and benefits of the lifestyle that are most important to them shift and evolve over time, everyone starts somewhere!
