PHYSICAL HEALTH
HEALTH IMPACT OF ANIMAL PRODUCTS
Meat and other animal products tend to be high in saturated fats and dietary cholesterol which can contribute to higher rates of obesity and a higher risk of heart disease. (1) In addition, meat and animal products often contain harsh chemical preservatives, antibiotics, carcinogens, and added hormones (2). Meat heavy diets are associated with a significantly higher level of risk of developing cancer (3). Diets heavy in animal products have also been linked to chronic lower respiratory disease, diabetes, stroke and cerebrovascular disease, and kidney disease.
HEALTH IMPACT OF PLANTS
Plant heavy diets naturally tend to have healthier macro ratios (4) (fats/ carbs/ protein) as well as more vitamins and minerals that the body can easily absorb. (5) Plants are high in fiber which improves digestive health. (6) Vegan and vegetarian diets have been shown to support a healthier gut microbiome (7) which has far reaching health effects. The low calorie density of many plant-centric meals helps with losing excess weight. (8) Poorly planned vegan diets may provide insufficient amounts of essential fatty acids, vitamin B12, iron, calcium, iodine or zinc (9)
NOTICEABLE PHYSICAL BENEFITS
Some of the more noticeable phsical benefits of a vegan diet include stronger nails and hair (10), whiter teeth, less fatigue (11), clearer skin (12), reduced bad breath and less body odor (13), better digestive health, and improved sexual health (14).
