MEDICAL EXPENSES
There are no studies that have been done comparing the healthcare costs of non-vegans to vegans. However, non-vegans are 15% more likely to develop cancer, 40% more likely to develop heart disease, and 100% more likely to develop diabetes (2)(3)(4) than vegans. Some insurance companies are even offering lower rates for vegans (5).
PREPARED VS UNPREPARED FOODS
Vegan or not, prepared foods tend to be more expensive. Vegan food especially sometimes.

The staples like grains, beans, lentils, vegetables, and fruits on the other hand can all be bought either fresh or in bulk and are very cost effective. Meat replacements are on the pricier end but are approximately the same cost as their meat equivalent.
